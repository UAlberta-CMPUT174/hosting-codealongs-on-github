Scenario:

Mr. Ratburn wants to assign seating in his classroom based on the first letter of students' names.

  • Students whose names start with letters A - M are on the left side of the classroom
  • Students whose names start with letters N - Z are on the right side of the classroom

Task:

You can help Mr. Ratburn assign a side to a student. Write a program that asks for a name and then determines and prints which side of the classroom the student will be seated.
