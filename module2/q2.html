Scenario:

A tray of chicken breasts from Costco weighs 2.32 kg. Carol consumed one-third of the packet. There are 23 grams of protein in each 6 oz chicken breast.

Task:

Write a program that determines and prints how much protein (in grams) Carol consumed.

Given:

  • 1 kg = 2.20462 pounds
  • 1 lb = 16 oz
