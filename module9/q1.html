Scenario:

Tara was working on her code in a physics lab. Recently, she found that some of her calculations were not working properly.

In particular, when dividing or multiplying numbers with repeating decimals, she found that introduced errors in her calculations.

This is because of the inaccuracy of floating point numbers. This error could potentially compromise the validity of her research.

Create a Rational number call that can help Tara with her research.

Think about what attributes your Rational class should have!

Problem:

Create your Rational class and add the appropriate attributes. Make sure to write your constructor function to initialize those attributes!
