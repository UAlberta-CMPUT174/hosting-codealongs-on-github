Scenario:

Sookie wants to make trout cakes for her Bistro. The recipe calls for 5 trouts. In order to catch fresh trout, she goes fishing in a nearby lake. Sookie wants to keep the trout that she catches and release any other fish back into the lake; however, she must abide by the local fishery regulations.

The fishery regulation in her town has set a daily catch limit of 7, which means that Sookie cannot have more than 7 fish in her possession in one day. Since the mortality rate of fish that are caught and released is high, there is also a daily catch and release limit of 3, which means that Sookie can catch and immediately release no more than 3 fish in one day.

The catch and release limit prevents Sookie from releasing all fish that are not trout, so she wants to stop fishing once she has enough fish for the trout cakes.

Task:

Create a program that helps Sookie keep track of how many trout she has caught without exceeding any limits. Your program should print:

  • 'Trout cakes' if Sookie has enough trout.
  • 'Seafood medley' otherwise
