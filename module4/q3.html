Scenario:

Sookie currently has 4 employees working at her bistro at the rate of $20 per hour. Currently, she has to manually enter the number of hours that each employee works per day in a week in a text file. Each line in the file starts with the employee's name, followed by the hours they've worked each day that week.

For example: 

	Sarah	7 4 3
	Bob 	9 2 7 5 
	David	8 8 7 8
	Mary 	6 5 7

Sookie is planning on expanding the bistro and hiring more employees. Therefore, she would like to automate the weekly payroll.

Task:

Help Sookie create a program that:

  • reads in a text file
  • computes and displays the total hours per week
  • computes and displays the weekly salary for each employee
  • finds the names of all employees who worked more than the average hours worked by all employees
Have the program display the information in the following matter: 

  Sarah	14	$280
  Bob	23 	$460
  David 	31 	$620
  Mary 	18	$360
  
  The average hours worked by all employees is 21.5. Bob, David worked more than 21.5 hours per week
